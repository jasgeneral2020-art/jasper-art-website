Artist Statement

My work moves between figuration, abstraction, memory, and emotional presence. I am drawn to forms that seem unstable, incomplete, or in the process of becoming. Through paintings and drawings, I explore figures, fragments, spaces, and gestures that hold tension between clarity and disappearance.
