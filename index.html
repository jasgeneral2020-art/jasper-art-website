Artist Statement

Each piece is one-of-a-kind, created purely from imagination and guided by intuition. The artist seeks not perfection but the emotional resonance hidden in strokes, shades, forms, and colors—leaning toward impressionistic expression. These works move between light-hearted humor, the bizarre, the dark, and the dreamy, born out of joy, passion, hope, and at times depression, anxiety, or even delirium. Themes return and evolve: living spaces and city edges; faces and figures; landscapes and odd little mash-ups that bring a smile. For the artist, the blank canvas is both terrifying and exhilarating, a place to lose oneself completely—and one where viewers often find themselves equally absorbed.
